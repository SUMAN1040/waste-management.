About Us

The ‘WASTE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM’ is a web application aimed to provide a digital way of complaining the concerns of general citizens to their relative municipalities.

  • Complaining about waste or garbage problems near their locality.
  • See their complain Report and check if the work is done! or not.
  • people can take different ideas regarding recycling of waste through this website.

Complaining about the waste problem encountered everyday to municipality is hefty process and waste management aims to make this process easier. With a simple handheld device with access to internet, user can use this platform complain their concerns to municipality . The automated system will redirect the complains . The municipality admins at the receiving side can acknowledge the reports which lets the users whether their complain is adddressed or not.

Guidelines implementation must for proper management of waste

A Lot of waste is generated every day and most of it is not effectively managed, this has a massive impact on the environment and human health. India produces 62 million tonnes of waste annually, with 70% collected, and only 12 million tonnes treated, while 31 million tonnes end up in landfills. The generation of municipal solid waste is expected to rise to 165 million tonnes by 2030 due to changing consumption patterns and rapid economic growth. For sustainability and environmental consciousness, our company stands as a beacon of innovation and responsibility in waste management. Our mission is simple yet profound: to connect local communities with companies eager to recycle and repurpose waste materials following the "GUIDELINES FOR MUNICIPAL SOLID WASTE MANAGEMENT."

  • 01 Disposal vs. Management

    Disposing solid waste out of sight does not solve the problem. The consequences of this practice are health hazards, pollution of soil, water, air & food,unpleasant surroundings, loss of precious resources that could be obtained from the solid wastes. That’s why it is essential to focus on proper management of waste all over the world . Solid waste management (SWM) is the application of techniques for orderly execution of collection, transport,processing, treatment and disposal of solid waste.SWM is not mere dumping rather a range of options including re-use, recycling, incineration with energy recovery, advanced landfill design and engineering and a range of alternative technologies. It aims at an overall waste management system which is the best environmentally, economically sustainable for a particular region

  • 02 Implementation problem

    One of the major waste management problem is the lack of adequate collection and disposal infrastructure as well as lack of proper collection, transportation, and disposal of waste which increases the risk of disease outbreaks and can cause social problems, public health threats, and environmental deterioration. The another problem is the resistance to adopting waste management procedures that can originate from various sources, including people, companies, and governments.

  • 03 Strengthening Waste Management Policies

    Implementing Advanced waste collection systems can greatly enhance waste management efficiency. Utilizing technology, such as asset tracking, optimizes waste collection routes and ensures timely pick-ups. Waste management companies should invest in waste management software to improve collection processes and reduce waste accumulation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Waste management regulations and disposal methods.

  • What types of waste materials does your service collect from households?

    Our service collects various types of waste materials, including general household waste, recyclables, organic waste, and in some cases, hazardous waste (with certain restrictions).

  • Can customers schedule pickups or is there a set schedule for waste collection?

    Customers can schedule pickups for large items or special waste materials, but regular waste collection follows a set schedule based on the location.

  • Can customers track the status of their waste collection requests or pickups?

    Yes, customers can track the status of their waste collection requests or pickups through our online portal or mobile app.

  • Are there any specific guidelines or regulations customers need to follow when preparing waste for collection?

    Customers are provided with guidelines on how to properly prepare waste for collection, including bagging trash, separating recyclables, and securing bulky items for pickup.

  • How do you work with waste disposal companies to ensure the proper treatment and disposal of collected waste materials?

    We work closely with waste disposal companies to ensure that collected waste materials are treated and disposed of in compliance with regulations and environmental standards, following established protocols for disposal and recycling.

  • Do you offer any specialized services for hazardous waste disposal?

    Yes, we offer specialized services for hazardous waste disposal, which require separate handling and transportation to designated facilities for safe disposal.
